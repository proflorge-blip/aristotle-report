About Aristotle

Our purpose is simple: to provide traders, researchers, and developers in the Sui ecosystem with clear, empirical data — free from hype, speculation, and trading signals.

Sources & Delivery

We source all metrics transparently from Pyth, DefiLlama, DeepBook, and Dune Analytics. These feed into our proprietary Logos Index, which distills six key on-chain metrics into a single, neutral measure of network health. Updates are delivered twice daily to Telegram at 7am and 7pm UTC.

Giving

In keeping with the spirit of philosophical inquiry, 3% of all subscription revenue is donated to Philosophy with Children International.

Aristotle is in early access. Founding subscribers receive preferred pricing and direct input on the product roadmap.
