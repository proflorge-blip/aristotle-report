The Logos Index

The ancient Greeks believed there were two complementary ways of thinking and learning: through mythos — mythical, fabulous narratives — and through logos — the logical and rational analysis of phenomena. In a market driven by narratives, Logos tracks what is real.

What it is

The Logos Index is a single score from 1 to 100 that reflects the overall health and steadiness of the Sui network independently of price. It combines five key on-chain metrics — each weighted and normalised against a 30-day baseline — into one calm, rational reading. A rising Index means the network is strengthening across multiple dimensions simultaneously. A falling Index means the opposite.

There are no labels. No bullish or bearish signals. No buy or sell recommendations. Just a number.

Methodology

The Logos Index is built from five inputs:

  • Active Addresses
  • Total Value Locked (TVL)
  • DeepBook Liquidity
  • SUI Price
  • Mean Reversion

Each metric is normalized against its 30-day rolling baseline. The normalized scores are then combined using proprietary weightings and dampened — blending today's reading with yesterday's — so the Index moves deliberately rather than reactively.

On Mean Reversion

This component measures how far the 24-hour price change deviates from its recent average. Calm sessions score higher. Extreme moves in either direction score lower. Network health is not the same as price excitement.

The Logos Index is exclusive to Aristotle: Sui Logos subscribers. Delivered twice daily — 07:00 and 19:00 UTC — directly to Telegram.
